What are Crypto Punks?

CryptoPunks were created by Larva Labs in 2017. The collection consists of 10,000 distinct characters that were randomly generated using a set of attributes. They are the first NFT series to catch popular interest, and one of the most actively traded. The highest price paid for a Crypto Punk was US$7.58 million on March 11, 2021! Since their creation, the traded volume for the collection has reached US$1.96 Billion.

Use this website to explore the collection by predicting which type of Crypto Punk the image represents, and if among its accessories, the image has glasses or not.
